Top three activities to do at Sint Maartensdijk

Go and fish for craps

Bring some robe and lure and catch them between the boulders of a small dam at Sint Maartensdijk baai.

Ask for Kleppertje

Rattle the letterbox and buy a bag of candy!

Visit the Pluimpot

Take a beautiful walk from the village through the woods and the Pluimpot to the beach.
