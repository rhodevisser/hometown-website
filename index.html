Visit Sint Maartensdijk

Enjoy this pitoresk small and maybe a bit boring smallcity in Zeeland a province in the Netherlands

Top three activities to do at Sint Maartensdijk

Go and fish for craps

Catch them between the boulders of a small dam at Sint Maartensdijk baai.

Ask for Kleppertje

Rattle the letterbox, maybe someone is home and you buy a bag of candy!

Visit the Pluimpot

Take a beautiful walk from the village through the woods and the Pluimpot to the beach.
